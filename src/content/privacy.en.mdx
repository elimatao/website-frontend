## 1. General Information
We do not use cookies, tracking tools, or contact forms on this website. This site is designed to be privacy-friendly.

## 2. Server Log Files
The website provider automatically collects and stores information in "server log files," which your browser automatically transmits to us. These are:
- IP address
- Date and time of the server request
- Browser type and version
- Operating system used
- Referrer URL
This data is processed based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate Interest) to ensure the technical stability and security of the website.

## 3. Your Rights
You have the right to information, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing regarding your personal data. Contact us at the address provided in the Legal Notice.
